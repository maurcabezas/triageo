# Mobile App Known Issues and Workarounds

This document lists known bugs in current and recent mobile app releases, their status, and available workarounds.

## iOS — App crashes on launch (v3.4.1 and earlier)

**Status:** Fixed in v3.4.2 (released 2026-05-28)
**Affected:** iOS 17.0–17.4 on iPhone 12 and later
**Workaround:** Update to v3.4.2 via the App Store. If update is unavailable in your region, clear app cache from iPhone Settings > General > iPhone Storage > [App Name] > Offload App, then reinstall.

## Android — Push notifications not delivered

**Status:** Under investigation
**Affected:** Android 14 on Pixel 7 and 8 series
**Workaround:** Enable background app refresh. Settings > Apps > [App Name] > Battery > Unrestricted.

## iOS — Dark mode text unreadable in settings screen

**Status:** Fix scheduled for v3.5.0
**Affected:** iOS 17 with system dark mode enabled
**Workaround:** Switch device to light mode temporarily.

## Reporting a new mobile bug

For mobile-specific bugs not listed here, collect the device model, OS version, and app version and create a bug report via the standard crash reporting process (see bug-001).
