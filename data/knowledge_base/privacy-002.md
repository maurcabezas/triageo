# Data Subject Access Requests (DSAR) — Data Portability

Customers may request a full export of their personal data under GDPR Article 15 (right of access) and Article 20 (data portability). This includes all data held about them in structured, machine-readable format.

## What is included in a DSAR export

- Account information (name, email, registration date)
- All content created or uploaded by the user
- Activity logs (login history, actions performed)
- Communication history with support
- Third-party connections and OAuth tokens (metadata only)

## Process

1. Verify the requestor's identity (email match + secondary verification).
2. Generate export via the internal data export tool.
3. Deliver export as an encrypted ZIP file via secure download link.
4. Complete within 30 days of verified request.

## Repeated requests

If a customer submits multiple DSARs within 12 months, the second request may be subject to an administrative fee or refused, with written justification, under GDPR Article 12(5).
