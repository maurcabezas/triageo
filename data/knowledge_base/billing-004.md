# Invoice and Receipt Requests

Customers may need invoices or VAT receipts for expense claims or accounting purposes.

## Downloading invoices self-service

Customers can download all past invoices from their account:
- Account Settings > Billing > Invoice history
- Click the download icon next to any charge

## Invoice not showing correct company details

If a customer needs an invoice with their company name, VAT number, or billing address:
1. Ask the customer to update their billing information in Settings > Billing > Billing details.
2. Once updated, future invoices will include the correct details.
3. For retroactive corrections on past invoices, a manual invoice can be issued — requires billing team action.

## VAT-exempt customers

Enterprise customers in eligible jurisdictions can apply for VAT-exempt status. They must submit their VAT registration number and certificate via the billing settings page. Processing takes up to 5 business days.

## Requesting a custom invoice format

Custom invoice formats (PO numbers, specific line item descriptions) are available for enterprise customers only. Contact the account manager.
