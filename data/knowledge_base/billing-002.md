# Subscription Cancellation and Downgrade Policy

Customers may cancel or downgrade their subscription at any time. This document covers how cancellations and downgrades are handled and what customers are entitled to.

## Cancellation

- Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing period.
- No prorated refunds are issued for time remaining in the period unless the customer is within the 14-day cooling-off period.
- After cancellation the account switches to a limited free tier; data is retained for 90 days before deletion.

## Cooling-off refunds

Customers who cancel within 14 days of initial subscription purchase are eligible for a full refund under EU consumer law.

## Downgrade

- Downgrades take effect immediately.
- If the customer paid annually, the difference is applied as a credit to the account — not refunded.
- Monthly downgraders are switched at the next renewal date.

## Reactivation

Cancelled accounts can be reactivated within 90 days with data fully restored. After 90 days, account data is permanently deleted and reactivation starts fresh.
