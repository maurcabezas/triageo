# Password Reset and Account Recovery

If a customer cannot log in because they forgot their password or lost access to their email, use this guide to help them recover their account.

## Standard password reset

1. Direct the customer to the login page and click "Forgot password".
2. A reset link is sent to the registered email address.
3. The link is valid for 2 hours.
4. If the email does not arrive, check spam/junk folders and ensure the customer is using the correct email address.

## Email address no longer accessible

If the customer no longer has access to their registered email:
1. Request identity verification (government-issued ID or last 4 digits of payment card on file).
2. If verified, update the email address in the admin panel.
3. Trigger a fresh password reset to the new email.

## Account locked after failed attempts

Accounts are locked after 10 consecutive failed login attempts. Lockout duration is 30 minutes. After 30 minutes the account unlocks automatically. Support can unlock manually via the admin dashboard if the customer cannot wait.

## Magic link login

As an alternative to password reset, support can issue a one-time magic link login for verified customers. Valid for 10 minutes.
