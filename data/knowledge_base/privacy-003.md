# Privacy Consent, Cookies, and Marketing Opt-Out

Customers have the right to withdraw marketing consent and opt out of non-essential cookies at any time.

## Marketing email opt-out

- Customers can unsubscribe from marketing emails using the unsubscribe link at the bottom of every marketing email.
- Unsubscribes are processed within 10 business days per CAN-SPAM.
- Transactional emails (receipts, security alerts, password resets) are not affected by marketing opt-out.
- If a customer reports receiving marketing emails after opting out, investigate and escalate to the marketing ops team.

## Cookie preferences

- Cookie preferences can be updated at any time from the cookie banner (click the floating cookie icon in the bottom-left).
- Essential cookies cannot be disabled — they are required for the service to function.
- Analytics and advertising cookies can be fully disabled.

## Do Not Track

The application respects the DNT browser header — analytics are disabled when DNT is detected.

## Data processing objection (GDPR Article 21)

Customers may object to data processing for legitimate interests. These objections must be escalated to the privacy-ops team for legal review.
