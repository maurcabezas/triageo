# Terms of Service Violations and Account Enforcement

This document describes the enforcement actions available to the trust-safety team and the process for handling ToS violations.

## Enforcement actions (in escalating order)

1. **Warning** — First minor violation. No service impact.
2. **Content removal** — Specific content or post removed without account action.
3. **Feature restriction** — Limit specific features (e.g., messaging, posting).
4. **Temporary suspension** — Account suspended for a defined period (1–30 days).
5. **Permanent ban** — Account permanently disabled. All data scheduled for deletion.

## Common violations and default actions

| Violation | Default action |
|---|---|
| Spam messaging | Warning + feature restriction |
| Fake reviews | Content removal + warning |
| Coordinated inauthentic behaviour | Suspension or permanent ban |
| Phishing or fraud | Permanent ban |
| Severe or repeated harassment | Suspension or permanent ban |

## Appeals

Customers who believe their account was incorrectly actioned may appeal within 30 days. Appeals are reviewed by a trust-safety team member who was not involved in the original decision.

## Routing

All enforcement decisions route to trust-safety. Do not apply enforcement actions without trust-safety review except for billing suspensions.
