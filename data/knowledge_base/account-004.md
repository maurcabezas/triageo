# Enterprise Plan — Features and Support SLA

Enterprise customers have access to dedicated support, custom configurations, and enhanced SLAs. This document describes entitlements and how to handle enterprise tickets.

## Enterprise support SLA

| Priority | Response time | Resolution target |
|---|---|---|
| Critical (P0) | 15 minutes | 2 hours |
| High (P1) | 1 hour | 8 hours |
| Medium (P2) | 4 hours | 2 business days |
| Low (P3) | 1 business day | 1 week |

## Enterprise-only features

- SSO (SAML 2.0 / OIDC)
- Custom data residency
- Dedicated account manager
- Priority engineering escalation
- Custom SLA agreements
- Bulk user management API
- Audit log export

## Escalating enterprise tickets

Enterprise customers must never be placed in the general queue. If a ticket from an enterprise customer is marked high or critical, page the on-call engineer immediately.

## Identifying enterprise customers

Enterprise accounts display an "Enterprise" badge in the admin panel customer view. Customer tier is also included in ticket metadata.
