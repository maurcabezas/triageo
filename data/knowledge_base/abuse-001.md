# Reporting Spam, Harassment, and Abuse

Users can report abusive behaviour, spam, or harassment through the in-app reporting tools or via a support ticket. This document explains how to triage and escalate these reports.

## Categories of abuse

- **Spam:** Unsolicited bulk messages, fake accounts, scraping activity
- **Harassment:** Targeted unwanted contact, threats, doxxing
- **Hate speech:** Content targeting protected characteristics
- **Impersonation:** Accounts pretending to be another person or brand
- **CSAM:** Zero-tolerance, mandatory escalation — see escalation procedure below

## Triage process

1. Acknowledge the report within 2 hours.
2. Review the reported content or account using the moderation dashboard.
3. Apply a temporary hold on the reported account if the content is clearly violating.
4. Document the case with screenshots before any action (evidence preservation).
5. Apply the appropriate enforcement action (warning, suspension, permanent ban).

## CSAM escalation

Any report involving child sexual abuse material must be escalated immediately to the trust-safety lead and the legal team. Do not review or store the content. Follow the mandatory reporting procedure.

## Routing

All abuse reports route to trust-safety. Severe cases (threats, CSAM, coordinated abuse) route directly to the trust-safety lead.
