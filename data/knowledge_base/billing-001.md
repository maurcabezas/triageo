# Duplicate Charge and Refund Policy

If a customer has been charged more than once for the same billing period or product, the duplicate charge must be refunded promptly.

## Identifying a duplicate charge

- Two charges with the same amount on the same or consecutive days
- Charge appearing after a failed payment retry
- Charge after a subscription was already cancelled

## Refund process

1. Confirm the duplicate using the billing dashboard (search by customer email or subscription ID).
2. Issue a full refund for the duplicate charge through the payment processor.
3. Refund timeline: 5–10 business days for credit/debit cards; 1–3 days for PayPal.
4. Send a confirmation email with the refund reference number.

## Edge cases

- If the customer was on a legacy plan that auto-renewed after a pricing change, issue the refund and flag for plan review.
- Do not issue more than one refund per billing cycle without manager approval.

## Routing

Billing disputes with amounts over $500 must be escalated to the billing-ops team lead.
