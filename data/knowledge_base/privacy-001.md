# GDPR Right to Erasure — Data Deletion Requests

Customers have the right to request deletion of all personal data under GDPR Article 17 (right to erasure) and CCPA. This covers account data, usage history, stored files, and any third-party data shares.

## Process

1. Customer submits a deletion request via support ticket or the in-app privacy portal.
2. Identity must be verified before processing begins.
3. Deletion is completed within 30 days (GDPR) or 45 days (CCPA).
4. A confirmation email is sent when deletion is complete.
5. Backups are purged within the next scheduled backup rotation cycle.

## Scope of deletion

- Account profile and credentials
- Billing history (retained for legal/tax purposes for 7 years per jurisdiction)
- Usage logs and analytics
- Uploaded files and generated content
- Third-party integration tokens

## Escalation

Privacy deletion requests must always be reviewed by the privacy-ops team. Do not deny or delay without legal sign-off.
