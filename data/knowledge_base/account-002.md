# Account Suspension and Ban Policy

Accounts may be suspended for policy violations, non-payment, or security reasons. This document explains the suspension types and how support should handle related tickets.

## Suspension types

| Type | Cause | Customer can self-recover |
|---|---|---|
| Billing suspension | Unpaid balance | Yes — update payment method |
| Security hold | Suspicious login detected | Yes — complete identity verification |
| Policy violation (soft) | Minor ToS breach | Requires support review |
| Policy violation (hard ban) | Severe abuse, fraud | No — escalate to trust-safety |

## Billing suspension

Customers on billing suspension retain all data but cannot access the service. Resolved automatically when a valid payment is received.

## Security hold

Triggered by unusual activity (login from new country, multiple failed 2FA attempts). Customer is sent a security verification email. Support can manually lift a security hold after verifying identity.

## Policy suspension

Do not lift a policy suspension without reviewing the violation. Check the moderation notes in the customer's admin profile. If in doubt, escalate to trust-safety.
